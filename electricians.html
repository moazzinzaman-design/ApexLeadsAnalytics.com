What Customers Need (Electrical Services)

Homeowners and businesses regularly search for electricians to handle essential and urgent electrical work. These jobs are high-value, frequent, and time-sensitive — making electrician leads some of the most profitable in the trades industry.

Common Electrical Services in Demand

  • Emergency Callouts: Electrical faults, power cuts, and urgent repairs
  • Fuse Board & Consumer Unit Upgrades: Modernizing old electrical infrastructure
  • Full Property Rewiring: New builds and major renovations
  • Lighting Installation: Interior, exterior, and decorative lighting
  • EV Charger Installation: Home and commercial charging stations (growing demand)
  • Outdoor Lighting: Garden lights, decking, landscape lighting
  • Socket & Switch Replacements: Updates and safety upgrades
  • Landlord Safety Certificates (EICR): Electrical testing and certification
  • Fault Finding & Diagnostics: Troubleshooting electrical issues
  • Testing & Inspection: PAT testing, condition reports

Market Insight: UK electrical services market worth £15B+. Average job value: £200-1,500. Emergency calls often £500-2,000. High-margin work with strong profitability and customer loyalty.
