Generate quality leads for professional carpet cleaning and upholstery services. Build a profitable carpet cleaning business with consistent customer demand.

What Customers Need

Homeowners and businesses need professional carpet cleaning to maintain their spaces. From regular maintenance to post-incident restoration, demand is consistent year-round.

Key Market Drivers

  • Regular carpet maintenance (quarterly/bi-annual)
  • Deep cleaning before special events
  • Pet odor and stain removal
  • Post-construction carpet cleaning
  • Move-in/move-out carpet cleaning
  • Commercial office cleaning contracts
  • Upholstery and furniture cleaning
  • Water damage and emergency restoration

Market Insight: Professional carpet cleaning market worth £1.5B+ in UK. Average customer spend: £120-300 per service. High repeat business with seasonal peaks.

How Fulfillment Works

We manage the entire customer relationship from initial inquiry through follow-up, ensuring every customer receives professional service and optimal scheduling.

Our Fulfillment Services Include

  • Customer Verification: We confirm property details and carpet information
  • Service Scope Assessment: Square footage, carpet type, stain severity, and special requirements documented
  • Quote Preparation: Optional: we prepare and send detailed quotes to customers
  • Appointment Booking: We coordinate schedules and book appointments
  • Pre-Service Confirmation: Reminders 24 hours before and access instructions
  • Payment Processing: Optional: we handle invoicing and payment collection
  • Follow-Up Scheduling: Automatic reminders for next maintenance cleaning (quarterly)

Typical Customer Journey

  • Customer requests carpet cleaning and provides details (area size, carpet type, issues)
  • We verify information and assess scope of work
  • Quote provided (if optional service) or appointment scheduled directly
  • Appointment booked at customer's preferred date/time
  • Pre-service reminder and access confirmation 24 hours before
  • Service delivery with professional results
  • Payment collected (if applicable)
  • Quarterly reminder for next maintenance cleaning
