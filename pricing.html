Pricing FAQ

Have questions about our pricing? Find answers to common questions below.

Do you have setup fees?

No. There are no setup fees, onboarding fees, or hidden charges. You start paying only when you receive your first lead.

Can I switch payment plans?

Yes. You can switch between payment models anytime. Simply contact us, and we'll help transition your account with no penalties.

What if a lead doesn't convert?

You still pay for the lead. However, we guarantee lead quality and responsive customers. Our leads come from real, verified inquiries.

Are there minimum purchase requirements?

No. With pay-per-lead, buy as few or as many leads as you need. With subscriptions, cancel anytime with no penalties.

How are leads delivered?

Instantly. You receive notifications via email, WhatsApp, or SMS within seconds of a customer inquiry. Real-time delivery means you respond first.

What service areas do you cover?

We operate across the UK. Lead prices may vary by location due to demand, competition, and service complexity in your area.

Can I get leads for multiple services?

Absolutely. You can receive leads for one service or multiple services. Each service has its own pricing and can be managed independently.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes. For subscription models and high-volume lead purchases, we offer custom pricing. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How do I track my spending?

Our dashboard provides real-time analytics. Track leads received, conversion rates, spending, and ROI all in one place. Detailed reports available.
