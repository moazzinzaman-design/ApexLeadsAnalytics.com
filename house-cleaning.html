What Customers Need

Homeowners are constantly searching for reliable, affordable house cleaning services. Whether it's weekly maintenance, deep cleaning, or move-in/move-out cleaning, the demand is consistent and profitable.

Key Market Drivers

  • Busy professionals need regular cleaning help (weekly/bi-weekly)
  • Seasonal demand spikes (spring cleaning, holiday preparations)
  • Post-construction and renovation cleaning needs
  • Move-in/move-out cleaning requirements
  • Deep cleaning between regular service visits
  • Eco-friendly and specialized cleaning preferences

Market Insight: 72% of homeowners outsource house cleaning due to time constraints. Average spend: £120-300/month per household.

How Fulfillment Works

Beyond lead generation, we provide complete fulfillment services - we manage the entire customer service process so you can focus on delivery.

Our Fulfillment Services Include

  • Customer Vetting: We verify customers are legitimate and ready to book
  • Booking Management: We schedule appointments and gather service preferences
  • Customer Communication: We handle pre-appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Quality Control: We collect feedback after service completion
  • Payment Processing: Optional: we handle invoice generation and payment collection
  • Dispute Resolution: We mediate any customer service issues

Typical Customer Journey

  • Customer fills out lead form (location, home size, service type, preferred date)
  • We verify and contact customer to confirm appointment
  • Schedule appointment and collect payment preferences
  • Pre-appointment reminder sent 24 hours before
  • Service is delivered
  • Post-service feedback collected
  • Future bookings scheduled
