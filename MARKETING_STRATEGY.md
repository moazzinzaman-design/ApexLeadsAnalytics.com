# Apex Leads Analytics - Marketing Strategy

A comprehensive marketing strategy to acquire trade professionals and homeowners for the platform.

---

## Executive Summary

**Goal**: Build a sustainable flow of both:
1. Trade professionals (supply) - Plumbers, electricians, cleaners, etc.
2. Homeowners (demand) - People looking for trade services

**Target Market**: UK homeowners and trade professionals in major cities

---

## Phase 1: Market Validation (Month 1-2)

### 1.1 Direct Outreach to Trade Professionals
**Cost**: £0-100 | **Effort**: High | **Priority**: HIGH

- Visit local trade supply shops (e.g., Screwfix, Travis Perkins)
- Hand out flyers to trade professionals
- Offer free sign-ups with no commitment
- Target: 20 local pros sign up

### 1.2 Local Business Partnerships
**Cost**: £0 | **Effort**: Medium | **Priority**: HIGH

- Partner with local estate agents
- Partner with letting agents
- Partner with property management companies
- Offer referral commission (e.g., £20 per successful referral)

### 1.3 Google My Business
**Cost**: £0 | **Effort**: Low | **Priority**: MEDIUM

- Claim and optimize Google My Business listing
- Get reviews from early customers
- Post regular updates about services

---

## Phase 2: Digital Marketing (Month 2-4)

### 2.1 SEO Strategy
**Cost**: £0-500/month | **Effort**: Medium | **Priority**: HIGH

**Target Keywords**:
- "[service] near me" - e.g., "plumber near me", "cleaner near me"
- "best [service] in [city]" - e.g., "best cleaner in London"
- "how to hire a [trade]" - informational content
- "[service] for home" - e.g., "pressure washing for home"

**Local SEO**:
- Create location-specific pages (e.g., /plumber/london, /cleaner/manchester)
- Build local citations (Yelp, Thomson Local, CityLocal)
- Get backlinks from local business directories

### 2.2 Content Marketing
**Cost**: £0-200/month | **Effort**: Medium | **Priority**: HIGH

**Content Types**:
- Blog posts: "10 signs you need an electrician", "How to clean a carpet properly"
- Guides: "How to hire a reliable plumber", "What to look for in a cleaner"
- Videos: Before/after cleaning, quick DIY tips
- Infographics: Cost comparisons, "How it works" flowcharts

**Content Calendar**:
- 2 blog posts per week
- 1 video per month
- Share on social media

### 2.3 Social Media Marketing
**Cost**: £0 | **Effort**: Medium | **Priority**: MEDIUM

**Facebook/Instagram**:
- Join local community groups (e.g., "Mums in [city]", "Local residents")
- Share before/after photos of work
- Run targeted ads to homeowners aged 25-55

**Instagram**:
- Post high-quality before/after photos
- Use relevant hashtags (#cleaning #plumbing #homeimprovement)
- Collaborate with home improvement influencers

**LinkedIn**:
- Connect with B2B prospects (property managers, landlords)
- Share industry insights
- Join relevant LinkedIn groups

### 2.4 Paid Advertising
**Cost**: £300-1,000/month | **Effort**: Medium | **Priority**: MEDIUM

**Google Ads**:
- Budget: £10-30/day initially
- Target: Homeowners searching for "[service] near me"
- Focus on service areas

**Facebook/Instagram Ads**:
- Target: Homeowners aged 25-60
- Retarget website visitors
- Lookalike audiences of customers

---

## Phase 3: Referral & Partnership Programs (Month 3-6)

### 3.1 Referral Program for Pros
**Cost**: £20-50 per referral | **Effort**: Low | **Priority**: HIGH

- Offer existing pros £20 credit for each successful referral
- Offer new pros 1 month free when referred by existing pro
- Track referrals with unique referral codes

### 3.2 Referral Program for Homeowners
**Cost**: £10-20 per referral | **Effort**: Low | **Priority**: MEDIUM

- Offer homeowners £10 credit for referring a friend
- Offer discount on next booking

### 3.3 Trade Association Partnerships
**Cost**: £0-500/year | **Effort**: Medium | **Priority**: MEDIUM

- Partner with trade associations (e.g., Federation of Master Builders)
- Offer exclusive discounts to members
- Get listed in their member directories

### 3.4 White-Label & Franchise Options
**Cost**: Development time | **Effort**: High | **Priority**: LOW (Long-term)

- Offer white-label solutions for other businesses
- Franchise the model to other regions

---

## Phase 4: Brand Building (Month 4-12)

### 4.1 Trust Signals
**Cost**: £0-200 | **Effort**: Low | **Priority**: HIGH

- Verified badges for professionals
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Money-back guarantees
- Industry certifications displayed

### 4.2 Case Studies
**Cost**: £0 | **Effort**: Medium | **Priority**: MEDIUM

- Document success stories from pros
- Show revenue growth for pros using the platform
- Testimonials from happy homeowners

### 4.3 PR & Press
**Cost**: £0 | **Effort**: Medium | **Priority**: LOW

- Write press releases about milestones
- Pitch to local newspapers
- Apply for startup awards

## Immediate Action Items (This Week)

1. **Create Google My Business profile** (2 hours)
   - Go to business.google.com
   - Claim/verify business listing
   - Add photos and accurate information

2. **Design flyer for trade professionals** (1 hour)
   - Use Canva or hire on Fiverr (£20)
   - Include: What platform does, free sign-up link, QR code

3. **Visit local trade shops** (4 hours)
   - Go to Screwfix, Travis Perkins, local builders merchants
   - Hand out flyers to trade professionals
   - Target: Talk to 20 pros, get 5 sign-ups

4. **Set up Facebook Business page** (1 hour)
   - Create business page
   - Add photos and description
   - Post first content

5. **Create first blog post** (2 hours)
   - Topic: "10 signs you need a professional cleaner"
   - Post on website blog
   - Share on social media

## Summary

The marketing strategy focuses on:
1. **Low-cost direct outreach** to get initial traction
2. **Local SEO** to capture search traffic
3. **Content marketing** to build authority
4. **Referral programs** to drive word-of-mouth
5. **Paid advertising** to scale once unit economics work

Start with direct outreach and local SEO, then add paid ads once you have proof of concept.

**Key Success Factors**:
- Focus on one city initially
- Get pro onboarding right
- Ensure quick lead response times
- Build trust through reviews
