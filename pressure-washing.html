What Customers Need

Property owners need regular exterior cleaning for driveways, patios, building facades, and more. This is a high-demand service with strong seasonality and consistent customer needs.

Key Market Drivers

  • Spring and summer outdoor property cleaning (seasonal peak)
  • Driveway stain and algae removal
  • Patio and deck cleaning for entertaining spaces
  • Building facade and roof cleaning
  • Post-construction debris removal
  • Property preparation before sale (curb appeal)
  • Commercial building maintenance
  • Fleet and vehicle cleaning services

Market Insight: 58% of property owners hire pressure washing 1-3 times per year. Average spend: £150-400 per project. Strong repeat business potential.

How Fulfillment Works

We manage the entire customer lifecycle so you can focus on delivering quality work and scaling your business.

Our Fulfillment Services Include

  • Lead Verification: We confirm customers are legitimate and serious about the work
  • Service Scope Definition: We detail exactly what needs to be cleaned (driveway, patio, building, etc.)
  • Quote Management: Optional: we collect quotes and manage the estimation process
  • Booking Coordination: We schedule appointments at times convenient for both parties
  • Pre-Service Communication: Weather confirmations, equipment notifications, access instructions
  • Payment Processing: Optional: we invoice and collect payment
  • Quality Assurance: Post-service photos and customer satisfaction checks

Typical Customer Journey

  • Customer submits service request (property type, area to clean, preferred date)
  • We verify customer details and property access
  • Schedule appointment and confirm time window
  • Pre-service reminder 24 hours before
  • Service delivery
  • Payment collected (if applicable)
  • Post-service feedback for continuous improvement
