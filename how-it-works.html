A simple way for local service businesses to get more customers

Apex Leads Analytics helps cleaners, detailers, gardeners, pressure washers, and other local service providers grow their business through a reliable, pay-as-you-go lead system.

Tell Us What Service You Offer

  • Gutter cleaning
  • Car detailing
  • Garden maintenance
  • Carpet cleaning

You can start with one service and expand to others as your business grows. We'll adjust your preferences anytime.

Sign Up With Apex Leads Analytics

Registration is quick and straightforward. We just need some basic information to get you started.

  • Your full name
  • Business details and service area
  • Your availability and response time
  • Preferred payment method
  • Lead delivery preferences

Quick Onboarding

Once verified, your account is live and ready to receive leads. We typically activate within 24 hours.
3

We Run Ads and Bring Customers to You

Our in-house marketing team handles all the heavy lifting. You never need to worry about running ads or building a website.

  • Facebook & Instagram targeted campaigns
  • Google Local Services Ads
  • TikTok and other emerging platforms
  • SEO-optimized service pages
  • Customer reviews and reputation management

Data-Driven Marketing

We continuously optimize campaigns based on conversion data to ensure you get high-quality, responsive leads.
4

You Receive Qualified Leads Instantly

Every lead is pre-qualified and contains all the information you need to close the job quickly.

  • Customer name and phone number
  • Email address and location
  • Specific service required
  • Job details (size, scope, urgency)
  • Budget expectations when provided

Real-Time Notifications

Receive leads instantly via email, WhatsApp, or SMS so you can contact customers before your competitors do.
5

Contact the Customer and Schedule Work

Now the work is in your hands. You contact the customer, provide a quote, and schedule the job.

  • Call or message within 24 hours for best results
  • Provide your quote and availability
  • Confirm the job details
  • Schedule your appointment
  • Complete the work and get paid by the customer

Support Available

Our customer success team is available to help if you need guidance on lead management or customer communication.
6

Pay Only for Results

Choose the payment model that works best for your business. No hidden fees, no long-term contracts.

  • Only pay when you receive a lead or complete a job
  • Choose from three flexible payment options
  • Cancel or modify your plan anytime
  • Clear, transparent pricing with no surprises

Risk-Free

We're confident in the quality of our leads. If you're not satisfied, we'll work with you to improve results or adjust your plan.
7
